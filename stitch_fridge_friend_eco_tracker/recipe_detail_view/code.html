Preparation

1

Prepare the Grains

Rinse the quinoa thoroughly under cold water. In a medium pot, combine quinoa and water. Bring to a boil, then cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer for 15 minutes.

2

Roast the Chickpeas

Toss chickpeas with olive oil, smoked paprika, and a pinch of salt. Spread on a baking sheet and roast at 400°F (200°C) until golden and crispy.

3

Massage the Kale

Remove stems from kale and tear into bite-sized pieces. Drizzle with a little olive oil and a pinch of salt. Use your hands to massage the kale until it softens and turns a darker green.

4

Assemble & Serve

Divide quinoa, kale, and chickpeas between bowls. Top with sliced avocado, halved cherry tomatoes, and a generous drizzle of tahini dressing.
